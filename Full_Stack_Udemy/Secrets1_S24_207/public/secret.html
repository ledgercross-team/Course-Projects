Movie Secrets

  • The sound of the Velociraptors communicating in Jurassic Park was made by recording tortoises mating.
  • In The Matrix, the code falling on the screen is actually a recipe for sushi.
  • The original cut of The Wolf of Wall Street was over 4 hours long.
  • In Django Unchained, Leonardo DiCaprio cut his hand on broken glass but stayed in character, and his real blood appears in the final film.
